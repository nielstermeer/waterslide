WaterSlide multiplexing demonstration

You might have been pointed here by someone for a demonstration of the multiplexing feature of the WaterSlide presentation program.

Below these paragraphs, you'll find two so called "iframes". These "inline frames" are used to embed another webpage into another. You might recognise these as the technology used to show advertisements, or the way Youtube embeds video's into webpages.

These Iframes (in this case) represent two devices. One of these could be a laptop hooked up to a beamer. The other one could be your smartphone. You could then use said smartphone to walk around and present, and letting the laptop take care of the beamer. You could also use the laptop for keyboard control, when necessary

Both of these Iframes are set up to be so called "masters" of the presentation. If you go to the next slide (arrow keys, or swipes) in one presentation, the other one will follow. There is also a slave option, which will cause the slide configured as a slave to follow the master presentation.
